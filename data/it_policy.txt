Passwords: Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, containing letters, numbers, and special characters.
Passwords: Passwords must not be reused across multiple accounts and should be reset every 90 days.
Passwords: Never share your passwords with anyone, including coworkers, managers, or IT support staff.
Passwords: Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) must be enabled on all corporate and email accounts.
VPN Usage: Employees working remotely must connect to the corporate network via the approved VPN.
VPN Usage: VPN access should only be used for legitimate work purposes and never for personal browsing.
VPN Usage: Employees must not bypass, disable, or alter corporate VPN security configurations.
Laptop Security: All company-issued laptops must have password-protected screensavers activated after 10 minutes.
Laptop Security: Keep your laptop secure in public and enable the full-disk encryption provided by the IT department.
Laptop Security: Lost or stolen laptops must be reported to the IT department immediately to lock/wipe data.
Laptop Security: Do not install unauthorized or unapproved software on your corporate laptop.
Laptop Security: Ensure operating system and antivirus updates are installed regularly and not disabled.
Email Rules: Company email accounts must only be used for official business and minimal, appropriate personal use.
Email Rules: Never send unencrypted confidential or sensitive information to external recipients.
Email Rules: Exercise extreme caution with email attachments and links from unknown or unexpected senders.
Email Rules: Automatic email forwarding to personal external email accounts is strictly prohibited.
Email Rules: Never include passwords, PINs, or sensitive login credentials in the body of an email.
Email Rules: Report all suspicious, phishing, or malicious emails to the IT helpdesk immediately.
General Rules: All company data stored on devices or networks is subject to routine monitoring and review.
General Rules: Failure to adhere to these IT policies may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
